Karolis, A Poem To You, From Yours Truly, Zac Holland

Zac Jack Lesley Holland, bold and bright, Found in Karolis a love that feels so right. Through days of laughter, nights of stars above, Their bond is steady, built on endless love.

In every word, in every gentle glance, They share a story, a perfect dance. Like rivers meeting, flowing side by side, Zac holds Karolis with a heart open wide.

Through every challenge, through every storm, Together they rise, together they warm. For in Karolis, Zac’s heart found its song, A love so true, where they both belong.

So here’s to their bond, so pure and true, Zac Jack loves Karolis in all that they do. A tale of devotion, of joy, and of trust, A love that will last, forever, it must.
